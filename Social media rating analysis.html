Exploratory Data analysis¶

Note that this data was collected by a survey that asked people to rate these social media platforms:

Youtube

Facebook

Twitter

Instagram

The rate is from 0 to 10 where 0 means that this plaform has a negative impact on their mental health and 10 means that it has a postive impact on their mental health
